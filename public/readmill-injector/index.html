Readmill Injector

Easily import books to Readmill from your favourite sites.

Readmill Injector plugin available as Chrome Extension or as a Firefox Add-On. Readmill Injector inserts 'Send to Readmill' buttons on Project Gutenberg, Feedbooks and all other websites where it can find epub, pdf or acsm files. This makes it really easy to send books to Readmill's Library and thus, easy to send to Readmill's iPad application.
